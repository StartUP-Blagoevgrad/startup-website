Our team

We are a determined team, formed by university students who are driven by their passion for entrepreneurship and strive for success. Our goal is to create educational and inspiring events.

What We Do

StartUP@Blagoevgrad is a student organization at the American University in Bulgaria, that organizes events with the aim of promoting the entrepreneurial spirit and supporting the development of the startup ecosystem in the country. The focus of these events is to bring together successful entrepreneurs and driven students who aspire to gain insights into the realities of building a respectable business.
